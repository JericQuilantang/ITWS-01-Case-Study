Vision of the University

NEUST is a locally responsive and internationally relevant and
recognized University of Science and Technology.

Mission of the University

To develop new knowledge and technologies and transform
human resources into productive citizenry to bring about
development impact to local and international communities.

GOALS

To equip ethical and up-to-to-date Information Technology
experts who are able to enhance the university's global competitiveness
in the service of its long-term mission and mission.

College Goals

Train and produce highly skilled professionals
equipped with moral and up-to-date knowledge
in IT who are globally competitive contributors to the
realization of University’s vision and mission.
